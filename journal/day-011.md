## 💻 Project of the Day
- **Name:** Blackjack (Capstone Project)
- **Description:**  
A command-line implementation of the classic Blackjack card game.  
The player competes against a computer dealer, aiming to get as close to 21 as possible without going over.

---

## 🤔 Challenges & Solutions

- **Challenge:** Managing game flow between player and dealer.  
  - **Solution:** Separated logic into phases using loops (player turn vs dealer turn).

- **Challenge:** Handling the Ace value (11 or 1) dynamically.  
  - **Solution:** Adjusted the score inside `calculate_score()` when total exceeded 21.

- **Challenge:** Determining game outcomes cleanly.  
  - **Solution:** Created a dedicated `compare()` function to centralize win/loss logic.

- **Challenge:** Avoiding overly complex code.  
  - **Solution:** Broke logic into small, readable functions.
